#### 🌱 Amplicon Week – Day 6 Wrap-Up: From Reads to Insights

**This wraps up five days of Amplicon Week 🎉**

Over the past week, we’ve walked through the complete lifecycle of amplicon data analysis—from raw sequencing reads to biological interpretation.

#### What we covered this week

Across five focused days, we explored:

● Wet-lab concepts behind amplicon sequencing and marker choice

● End-to-end QIIME2 workflows for 16S, ITS, 18S, 12S, and COI

● Advanced visualization and statistics using Microeco in R

● Functional prediction from amplicon data (PICRUSt2, Tax4Fun2, FAPROTAX)

● Web-based microbiome exploration using easy16S

If you’ve followed along, you now have multiple complementary ways to analyze amplicon datasets:

● beginner-friendly GUIs

● reproducible command-line pipelines

● flexible R-based visualization and statistics
